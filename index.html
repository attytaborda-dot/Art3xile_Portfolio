About Me

Hello, my name is José Antonio Taborda Morán (JATM). I'm a Venezuelan digital artist based in Barcelona, Spain, specializing in character design, concept art, and visual storytelling for games, animation, and editorial media.

I've always loved drawing, even though I wasn't always able to. In 2022, I decided it was time to pick up the brush and draw the path I wanted—to pursue art professionally rather than wait for the perfect moment.

Personally, I believe art should tell stories capable of healing, uniting, and even highlighting reality in order to not only entertain but also raise awareness. This is something I've tried to integrate into my work.

I define myself as a person who strives to improve and grow to create the best possible work I can. For this reason, I'm always open to new challenges and collaborations that can enhance my skills in the creative industry.
